# Product Guide: Sprint Predictor

## Target Audience
- **Professional Track and Field Coaches:** Empowering coaches with data-driven insights to refine training strategies and monitor athlete progress.
- **Competitive Sprint Athletes:** Providing athletes with a clear understanding of their performance metrics and potential.

## Core Features
- **Video-Based Sprint Analysis:** Advanced tools to extract key metrics like ground contact time and stride length directly from video footage.
- **Performance Prediction:** Leveraging historical data and training trends to forecast future race results and potential.
- **Comparative Analysis:** Benchmarking athlete performance against established standards and historical records.
- **Athlete Race Analyses:** Detailed breakdowns of individual races to identify strengths and areas for improvement.

## Primary Goals
- **Optimize Training Programs:** Using performance insights to tailor training for maximum effectiveness.
- **Accurate Race Time Predictions:** Providing reliable forecasts for upcoming competitions.
- **Centralized Performance History:** A single source of truth for all athlete performance data and visualizations.

## Visual Identity & Design
- **Modern & Minimalist:** A clean, focused interface that prioritizes data clarity.
- **High-Contrast Data Visualizations:** Using bold visuals to make complex performance metrics easy to interpret at a glance.

## Platform Strategy
- **Web-Based Desktop Optimization:** Designed for deep analysis on larger screens, perfect for use trackside on laptops or tablets.
- **Mobile-Responsive Access:** Ensuring quick data entry and status checks are seamless on mobile devices.
